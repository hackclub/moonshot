# Florida Half‑Day Adventure

We’re in Florida for a half‑day focused on space exploration and theme‑park thrills.

## What is the plan?

Choose one streamlined option based on interest and time.

## Option A: Kennedy Space Center (Half‑Day)

- Start at Space Shuttle Atlantis + Shuttle Launch Experience (60–90 min)
- Walk the Rocket Garden (15–20 min)
- Explore Gateway: The Deep Space Launch Complex (30–45 min)
- If time remains, see the Apollo/Saturn V Center; skip the full bus tour unless you have >2 extra hours

Buy timed‑entry tickets in advance. Details: [Kennedy Space Center Visitor Complex](https://www.kennedyspacecenter.com).

## Option B: Universal Orlando (Half‑Day)

Focus on 3–4 headline attractions:

- The Wizarding World of Harry Potter (Hogsmeade or Diagon Alley)
- Hagrid’s Magical Creatures Motorbike Adventure or Jurassic World VelociCoaster
- One headliner at Universal Studios Florida (e.g., The Mummy)
- Use Single Rider or Express to minimize waits

Ticket info and hours: [Universal Orlando Resort](https://www.universalorlando.com).
